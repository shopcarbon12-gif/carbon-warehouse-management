Chainway DeviceAPI (required for built-in UHF on C72 / c72e, etc.)
- Not redistributed here. Request the Android SDK for your exact model from Chainway support.
- Copy into android/app/libs/:
  - DeviceAPI / rscja *.jar (or AAR) matching the firmware
  - Any bundled lib*.so for your ABI (armeabi-v7a / arm64-v8a) into jniLibs if the vendor instructs you to
- Rebuild the app. Without these, Settings shows antenna power N/A (native UHF never links).
- Barcode: if the physical scan trigger still does nothing, open the device “Scanner” / “Keyboard Emulator” utility and set output to broadcast intent or keyboard wedge with suffix CR/LF; CarbonWMS listens for common OEM decode broadcasts and for wedge text with newline.
